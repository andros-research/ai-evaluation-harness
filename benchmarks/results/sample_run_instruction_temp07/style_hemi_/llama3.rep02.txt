The office was still. I sat at my desk, coffee cup in front of me. The only sound was the hum of the air conditioning and the occasional rustle of papers on someone else's desk. It was early, before most people got here. I checked my watch, saw it was 7:45. I took a sip of coffee, felt the bitter taste spread through my mouth. The lights were bright, but the room still looked dim. I pulled out some files to review, began to read.
